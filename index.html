Repair Café

Urban gardening initiative transforming unused spaces into community gardens

Sustainability Learning Skills Development Utrecht

Description

Green Community Gardens is an urban gardening initiative that transforms unused spaces into vibrant community gardens. We work with local councils and landowners to secure unused plots and engage residents in creating sustainable, productive green spaces.

Our gardens provide fresh produce for local food banks and community kitchens, while also offering educational opportunities for schools and community groups to learn about sustainable gardening practices.

Skills Development

  • Gardening and horticulture skills
  • Sustainable agriculture practices
  • Community leadership and organization
  • Environmental education

Participation Options

  • Volunteer gardener (no experience needed)
  • Workshop leader or educator
  • Community outreach
  • Donation of tools, seeds, or materials

Sarah Johnson

Community Garden Coordinator

Sarah has been organizing community gardens for over 8 years, with a background in horticulture and community development. She's passionate about making green spaces accessible to all urban residents.
